About Me
Purpose-Driven. People-Focused.

I'm a communications and marketing professional with over 15 years of experience in ministry, nonprofit, and faith-based organizations. I specialize in building campaigns that connect with real people — not just metrics.

My work spans broadcast television scripts and grant writing to multi-channel marketing strategies and community newsletters. I've served churches, nonprofits, and community organizations across the Kansas City area and beyond.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Communication from the University of Texas at Arlington, and I bring both the creative instincts and the data literacy to back up every campaign I run.
